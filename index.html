You want to build & launch a new product.

But you are unsure about where to start.



Start with a blueprint.

Is there a market?

There are some key questions you need to answer before deciding to build. Is there a market for what you want to build? Is there a realistic path for you to take this product to market given your resources? Can you sustain demand? How much demand can you sustain given your resources?

Will they buy?

The hard, cold, reality is that what you envision in your mind (at Day 1), isn't always what the market responds to. How do you validate what you envision your product to be? How can you get certainty that what you invest in to build, is what the market will pay for? We will help you walk through that.

What will you build?

Your initial product doesn't have to be a full blown app. It could be a simple landing page. It could be a PDF. It could be a series of videos or audio files. Yet, it could also be an app. What you build should be driven by what your potential customers want. We will help you figure that out.

To Do or Not To Do?

There are many risks with building and launching a product. The first risk is building something that there is no market for, or for a market you can't reach. Once you get past those risks, then you have to build the right product and sell it properly. But figuring out if there is a market for what you are building, is the #1 risk you should be focused on at the onset. I will help you to figure this out, so you can make the first key decision you will face. After all, if you go ahead, you will likely be working on it for the next few years -- so you may as well take care of this risk as early as you can.


The truth of the matter is, you may be approaching the problem wrong. This problem may not be for you. Isn't it better you discover that during the planning process, than after many years and hundreds of thousands of dollars later?

Germination Phase

Product Discovery

You have some domain expertise and perhaps a client list, but don't know what product to build. The best products solve problems. I will work with you to discover good product ideas based on your unique value proposition.

Product Validation

Here you have a product idea, but you need to know three things:

  • 1) Is there a market for this product?
  • 2) Can I reasonably reach this market?
  • 3) Is this market big enough to justify my attention?

Note: This phase includes $500 in paid ads directed to some representation of the product (aka, real market feedback).

Evolution Phase

Product Roadmap

Growth Tactics

Here you have a validated idea and potentially an existing product. I will work with you to develop your product roadmap - which includes 3 aspects: Developing your financial model (Business Model), Architecture Model (Technical Infrastructure), High-Level Strategy (Company Direction).

We will also develop Growth Tactics -- specific tactics you can use to either get up to 10,000 users/month or $100K/year. This is naturally dependent on your execution, but we will give you a blueprint and framework for how to think about different ways to achieve this, specific to your unique skillset.
